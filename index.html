Americano

An Americano has a clean, smooth taste with a moderate strength, making it a versatile choice for coffee lovers who prefer less intensity than espresso but more flavor than regular drip coffee.

Latte

The name Latte (from Italian “milk”) speaks about the main ingredient of the drink. It is recommended to take fat milk - at least 3.2%. In addition to it, milk foam.

Espresso

Espresso - black coffee made in a coffee machine or a special coffee machine, where hot water is passed through pressed ground beans at a pressure.

Cappuccino

A cappuccino is a classic Italian coffee made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. It has a stronger coffee flavor than a latte and is often topped with cocoa powder or cinnamon for added flavor.

Macchiato

An espresso macchiato consists of a shot of espresso "stained" or "marked" with a small amount of milk foam. This drink highlights the bold flavor of espresso with just a hint of creaminess.

Mocha

A mocha is a chocolate-flavored coffee drink made with espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup or powder.
